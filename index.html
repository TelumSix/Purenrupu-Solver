Purenrupu (Pure Loop) Solver

Click cells to toggle shading. The loop must avoid shaded cells.

Rules

  • Draw a single continuous closed loop (it must start and end on the same cell).
  • The loop passes through every unshaded cell exactly once.
  • Shaded cells are walls, the loop must not enter them.
  • The loop travels horizontally and vertically (no diagonals).
